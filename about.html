About Fourth & AI

Where the research on machine learning meets the line of scrimmage — in plain English.

Fourth & AI is a popularization project: it takes two scholarly articles on real-time, explainable artificial intelligence in American football and rewrites their findings for fans who already live in the analytics layer — the Next Gen Stats checkers, the PFF-podcast listeners — but who don't read journals for fun.

The goal isn't to dumb the research down. It's to keep the rigor and lose the jargon: same ideas, told the way a good analytics column would tell them.
